ABOUT

Penny Juice is a family owned business. We make premium juice concentrates your kids will love! We've designed them especially for childcare, daycare, preschool, and extended daycare.

WHY BUY?

Buyers will love the price! Kids will love the flavors! Parents will love the healthy ingredients! All the juice concentrates that we sell are compliant with the USDA and FDA guidelines. You don't need to refrigerate the concentrates before or after opening them.

PRODUCT

Penny Juice concentrates provide a rainbow of exciting flavors that all kids can enjoy! We offer 15 different flavors including 7 color free flavors. Each case includes 6 half gallon bottles of your choice!
